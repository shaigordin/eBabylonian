## Grammatical Focus (Reference to Appendix)
While reading letters, look out for:
- Pronouns, Pronomial Suffixes (especially 1st and 2nd person)
- Imperatives and Precatives
- Temporal and Causal Clauses (e.g., *inūma*, *aššum*)
